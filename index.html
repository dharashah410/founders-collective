FOUNDERS COLLECTIVE

Who are we?

We are a Collective of founders, by founders, for founders. We foster a collaborative approach to help founders and would be founders, enabling them to have multiplier effects to maximize the initiative of a single individual.

We are the people who we are looking for.

Who are we looking for?

  • People who have startup ideas.
  • People who want to work in startups.
  • People who are interested in studying startups.
  • People who want to work with startups.
  • People who want to invest time with startups.
  • People who want to invest capital with startups.
  • People who have previously run a startup.
  • People who want to support a startup.
  • People who want to mentor a startup.
  • People who are running a startup.

Who is a founder?

A person who has taken an initiative whether for profit or non profit to serve an economic or societal need.

Our Thesis

Instead of reinventing the wheel, why not collaborate and help build the next big spaceship? We believe in action. Tell us what you need and why you feel you need it, and we may help you meet it.
